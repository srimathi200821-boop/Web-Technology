❓ Quick FAQs

How long does delivery take? ▾
We deliver within 30 minutes across Chennai. During peak hours, it may take up to 45 minutes.
Can I cancel my order? ▾
Yes! You can cancel within 5 minutes of placing the order. After that, cancellation may not be possible.
Do you offer refunds? ▾
Yes, we offer full refunds for cancelled or incorrect orders. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
Is there a minimum order value? ▾
Minimum order is ₹99 for delivery. Orders above ₹299 get free delivery!
