Our Story

Born from Passion,
Built on Flavour

SpiceMix started in 2018 when Chef Anand Rajan — a trained culinary artist from Chennai — decided to bring together the best of South Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines under one roof.

What began as a 12-table restaurant in Anna Nagar quickly grew into Chennai's go-to destination for multi-cuisine dining. Today, with 30-minute delivery and 350+ dishes, we bring the restaurant experience to your doorstep.

"Food is not just fuel — it's an emotion. At SpiceMix, every dish is made with the love of a home-cooked meal and the craft of a professional kitchen." — Chef Anand Rajan, Founder
